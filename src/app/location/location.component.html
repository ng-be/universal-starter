Holiday Inn Ghent Expo

Maaltekouter 3 - 9051 Ghent, Belgium

Holiday Inn Ghent Expo is conveniently located next to the E40 (Brussels-Ostend) and the E17 (Amsterdam-Paris) motorways.

Hop on the tram, 100 meters from Holiday Inn Ghent Expo, for a 20-minute ride into historic Ghent with its wealth of museums, medieval streets and 12th-century architecture. You can book a boat trip on the canal and sample locally brewed beer, or take a 30-minute drive to visit the cities of Brussels and Bruges.
