Social Media Challenge

Post your most original picture of you and/or your team showing the NG-BE logo and win a nice discount!

Rules to participate

  • Take a picture of people, or your favorite pet if you want :) showing the NG-BE logo
  • Post it on social media ( Facebook, Twitter or LinkedIn ) before 1/08/2018
  • Tag NG-BE in the post so that we are aware of your submission. Make sure your post is public so that we can review it.
  • The 3 most original pictures win a 25% discount on a workshop or conference ticket
  • Submissions are selected objectively by the members of the NG-BE core team
  • The 3 winners are announced on the 10th of Augustus 2018.
