Social Media Challenge

Post a picture to showcase NG-BE and qualify to win a 25% discount on your conference or workshop ticket!

Rules to participate

  • Take a funny or happy picture of yourself, someone else or even your favorite pet, showcasing the NG-BE logo.
  • Post your picture on social media ( Facebook, Twitter or LinkedIn ) before August 1, 2018.
  • Use #ngbe2018 or @ngbeconf in your post so that we are alerted of your submission. Make sure your post is public so that we can review it.
  • The 3 most original pictures win a 25% discount on a workshop or conference ticket.
  • Submissions are rated democratically by the members of the NG-BE core team.
  • The 3 winners are announced on the August 10, 2018.
